First year CSE PhD student @UMich

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) program at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor , and a member of the Language and Information Technologies Lab where Professor Rada Mihalcea advises me.

My research interest mainly lies in understanding and interpreting images and videos using natural languages. Therefore, my interest incorporates interdisciplinary fields within artificial intelligence involving natural language processing, computer vision, and emotion perception.

As much as I enjoy experiencing and learning new things, I love writing about them too. Feel free to check out my blog articles about AI, math, and other exciting topics!
